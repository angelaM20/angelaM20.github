About me

Currently I am studying at the Faculty of Computer science and Engineering. I just finished the first year of my studies. I graduated from Josip Broz-Tito High School with two diplomas: The State Matura and the International Baccalaureate.

My greatest passion in life besides my collage education is to travel and experiance the world, that is why I am making a blog about my travels so far.

Lifestyle

In my point of view, the life of a 20-year-old girl is an exciting journey of self-discovery and personal growth. She is full of energy, enthusiasm, and a thirst for new experiences. Every day is a chance to explore the world around her, whether it's through travel, trying new foods, or engaging in various activities. She is passionate about her interests, whether it's pursuing her studies, honing her artistic skills, or immersing herself in a particular hobby. She values her friendships and spends quality time with her close circle, enjoying fun outings, movie nights, and deep conversations. She also takes care of her well-being by prioritizing self-care, engaging in regular exercise, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. While she may face challenges and uncertainties, she approaches them with resilience and a positive mindset. Overall, her 20s are a time of adventure, self-expression, and creating a foundation for a fulfilling future.
